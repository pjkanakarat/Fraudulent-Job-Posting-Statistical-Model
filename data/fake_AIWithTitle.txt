Bachelor’s degree (or in progress) in Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or related field
Experience with C or C++ programming
Familiarity with microcontrollers (e.g., STM32, ESP32, or similar)
Understanding of basic digital circuits and signal processing
Ability to debug hardware/software integration issues
Preferred Qualifications

Exposure to robotics frameworks (ROS/ROS2)
Knowledge of Python for tooling and testing
Experience with oscilloscopes or logic analyzers
